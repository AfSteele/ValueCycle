TYPES OF INFORMATION COLLECTED

Upon registration with the Services, we will collect both Personal Data and non-personal information and/or data. “Personal Data” includes any information or data that identifies you as an individual or relates to an identifiable individual, for example: your name, postal address, telephone number, email address, credit card information, geolocation, certain device information, and IP address. If you submit any Personal Data relating to other people to us or to our service providers in connection with our Services, you represent and warrant that you have the authority to do so and to permit us to use the information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

We will collect and use Personal Data when you voluntarily provide it to us, with the exception of your IP address and other user data that may be automatically captured when you access our Services. In order to use some portions of our Services, a user must first complete a registration form or provide an email address that will require disclosure of Personal Data. In addition, and in order to contact us by email, you must provide an email address.

We automatically collect and aggregate certain Personal Data generated from your use of our Services, such as a unique identifier (unconnected to your account login information or IP address), and related analytics and usage information that we use to track usage of our Service, track trends in our data and Services, administer the Services, and improve our users’ experiences. When we aggregate such Personal Data, we do not combine these types of Personal Data with other non-aggregated Personal Data. If we do combine these two, we treat the data as Personal Data.

USE OF INFORMATION

ValueCycle Health will use the Personal Data collected for internal business purposes that may include, but are not limited to, processing your transactions, establishing the identity of account holders, customer service, and providing you the Services. We may also use that Personal Data to improve and customize our content and Services.

We do not sell Personal Data to third parties, but we may share your Personal Data with third parties in order to fulfill your requests, such as payment and shipment of products, and otherwise support your use of the Services. We may share or sell aggregated Personal Data, demographic information, and other Services usage data with our business partners, sponsors, advertisers, and affiliates of the Company but prohibit use of such information for direct marketing. For example, we might inform third parties regarding the number of customers or clients utilizing our Services whose are located is in a certain geographic region. Or we might also inform a third party (that may or may not be promoting our Services on our behalf) that 45% of the users of our Services visit our website more than 3 times per week. Depending on the circumstances, we may or may not charge them for this information.

We will not disclose any information about your usage of our Services to unaffiliated third parties, except as necessary to enhance customer experience, service the legal agreement under which you are provided access to and the benefit of our Services or to meet our contractual obligations to third parties. Additionally, we also reserve the right to disclose your Personal Data if we are required to do so by law, regulation, subpoena, court order, or in the good faith belief that disclosure of the information and/or data is reasonably necessary to comply with legal process, to respond to claims, or to protect or advance the rights, property, safety, or well-being of the Company, its employees or contractors, customers, or the public.

If you have questions about or need further information concerning the basis on which we collect and use your Personal Data, please contact us using the contact information provided above.

When we disclose Personal Data as permitted under this Privacy Policy, we enter into a contract that describes the purpose and requires the recipient to keep that Personal Data confidential and use only for performance of the contract, and not for any other purpose.

If you request to exercise your rights under the GDPR, we may require verification of your identity before we respond to any such request. If you are entitled to these rights, you may exercise the following rights with respect to your Personal Data that we collect and store:

  • The right to withdraw consent to data processing at any time;
  • The right of access to your Personal Data;
  • The right to request a copy of your Personal Data;
  • The right to correct any inaccuracies in your Personal Data;
  • The right to erase your Personal Data;
  • The right to data portability, meaning to request a transfer of your Personal Data from us to any other person or entity as chosen by you;
  • The right to request restriction of the processing of your Personal Data; and
  • The right to object to processing of your Personal Data.

Any requests to exercise the above-listed rights may be made to: privacy@valuecycle.app. If you are an EEA resident, you have the right to lodge a complaint with a Data Protection Authority about how we process your Personal Data at the following website: https://edpb.europa.eu/about-edpb/board/members_en.

ValueCycle Health endeavors to comply with GDPR including, but not limited to, (a) protecting your right to have Personal Data collected only after you have specifically taken action to approve the collection of your Personal Data, (b) the right to easily access a mechanism to withdraw that approval and have your Personal Data promptly removed from our proprietary internal database, (c) using industry standard best practices to maintain care, custody, and control over the Personal Data we collect, and (d) reporting as prescribed by GDPR any breach of Personal Data within 72 hours of our discovery of such breach. ValueCycle Health believes the nature and scope of the Personal Data it collects does not require the designation of a GDPR Data Protection Officer. The Company processes Personal Data only on a lawful basis.
